Biography


Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, also known as APJ Abdul Kalam, was an Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Kalam came from a humble background and had to support his family from a young age.He started his career as a scientist at the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).In 2002, Kalam was elected as the President of India, becoming the first scientist to hold the position.Kalam passed away on July 27, 2015, while delivering a lecture at the Indian Institute of Management Shillong.
